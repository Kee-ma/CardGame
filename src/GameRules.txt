How to win:
Be the last team standing
A team is eliminated when all players on that team are defeated.

Supported game sizes:
2v2, 3v3

Each player has:
Points - points needed for survival, acts like health.
Points Rules
Points can increase up to max (depending on class)
 Points can decrease, but not below 0 (death)
 
Turns rotate by team:
Example (2v2):
 Team A Player 1
 Team A Player 2
 Team B Player 1
 Team B Player 2
 ~ repeats
 
Classes:
Tanks
Max Points: 50
 Take 30% reduced damage from damage cards
 Taunt: forces enemies to target Tank
 Taunt Cooldown: once every 2 turns
 
Warriors
Max Points: 25
 Deal +40% damage from damage cards
 Deal +80% damage vs Mages
 
Mages
Max Points: 30
 Damage uses base card values
 Take +40% damage from Warriors
 Healing effects using healing cards are doubled
 
Special Ability System:
Players charge a damage bar.
Deal 25 total damage --> unlock ability
Using ability resets bar to 0

Warrior Special Ability — Critical Hit
Next attack deals 3× damage

Mage Special Ability — Team Heal
Heals each teammate for 10 HP

Tank Special Ability — Skip Ability
Enemy team skips next full turn cycle
Tank team gets 2 consecutive rotations
 
Two decks of cards:
Mixed Deck
Damage Deck

Mixed deck card chances:
Point card — 26%
The Player who plays it gains points (2–6 points).

Healing card — 18%
 Heals the Player and each teammate by 3 Health Points.
 
Antidote card — 10%
 Cures a poisoned teammate.
 
Enhancement card — 7%
 Must be placed with another card to enhance that card’s ability by 80%.
 
Thief card — 12%
 Steals a card from another Player and places it in their hand. Costs 1 point to play.
 
Health Points Buff card — 10%
 Increases the player’s max health points by 10%.
 
Damage Buff card — 12%
 Increases the player’s future damage by 15%.
 
Inherit card — 3%
 Lets a player inherit a dead teammate’s cards.
 
 
Damage deck card chances:
Attack Card — 35%
 Deals 3–6 damage to an opponent.
 Player gains 1–2 points.
 
Freeze Card — 18%
 Freezes an opponent for one turn.
 Also deals 1–3 damage.
 Player gains 1–2 points.
 
Poison Card — 27%
 Deals 2 damage every turn until cured by Antidote.
 
Team Damage Card — 20%
 Deals 1–3 damage to all enemy players.
 
Turn Actions:
On a turn, players choose ONE:
Play a card from hand
Draw from Mixed Deck (cannot play on that turn)
Draw from Damage Deck (cannot play on that turn)
